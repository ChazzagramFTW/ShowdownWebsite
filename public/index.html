Welcome to Showdown

This is a 32-player event where 8 teams of 4 battle it out in an assortment of minigames to gain the most points. There are 7 games total to reach the most points.

This event aims to bring together creators of any size to battle it out, viewers can watch their favourite creators play in main events or help raise money for charity in special community events!

The Games

Our games aim to create a unique and enjoyable experience for all players, combining competitive elements with fun innovative additions. Our games focus on a variety of different skills including movement, pvp ability, puzzle solving, and teamwork. Some games combine different skills.
