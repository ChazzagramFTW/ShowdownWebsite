Genesis is here

Welcome to Showdown Genesis

We are creating more opportunities for players from across the community to compete in MC Showdown.

Applications for Showdown Genesis are open to everybody, separate to the Main Roster events this event will run alongside the original Seasons of MC Showdown.

MC Showdown will now be split into two events. MC Showdown will continue from its current season onwards: MC Showdown: Season Three.. while Showdown Genesis will be a fresh start: MC Showdown: Genesis One.

Genesis will run alongside the main event, with games and new features and additions coming to new Seasons of MC Showdown also crossing over to Genesis.
